What VIB3 Trades does

VIB3 Trades is a mobile investing app that helps everyday investors act on two things that are hard to do by hand: follow the publicly disclosed trades of politicians and hedge funds, and use AI-assisted analysis to make timely, disciplined decisions.

  • Copy & mirror the publicly disclosed trades of members of Congress (STOCK Act filings) and hedge funds (13F filings).
  • AI strategy that analyzes real-time market data and sentiment and proposes trades, with strict, user-defined risk limits.
  • Paper trading mode so you can practice risk-free with a simulated balance before committing real money.
  • Connect your own brokerage — your funds stay at your broker; the app never holds your money.

How it helps you

You stay in control. Set your own risk limits, choose whether to approve every trade or let it run within your limits, and start in paper mode until you trust it. Everything happens in your connected brokerage account — VIB3 Trades is the tool, you are the investor.
