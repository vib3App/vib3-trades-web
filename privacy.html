Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VIB3 Trades ("VIB3 Trades," "we," "us"), operated by Docs Marketplace LLC, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide when registering: name, email address, and authentication details (including via Google or Apple sign-in).
  • Brokerage connection data: when you connect a brokerage account, we receive read access to your balances, positions, and the ability to place orders you authorize. We connect to your broker through trusted third-party aggregators (including SnapTrade). We never receive or store your brokerage login password, and we never take custody of your funds — your money stays at your broker.
  • App usage and settings: your trading preferences, risk limits, and the actions you take in the app.
  • Device and technical data: device identifiers and basic diagnostic information used to operate and secure the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service: display your portfolio, generate analysis, and place the trades you configure or approve.
  • To secure your account and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the Service — including brokerage aggregators (such as SnapTrade) to connect your account, cloud infrastructure providers, and analytics/communication tools — and only as needed to provide the Service or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption of sensitive credentials in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

5. Your Choices

You may disconnect any linked brokerage at any time, and you may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

8. Contact
